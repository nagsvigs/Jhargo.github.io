Explore Jharkhand — Top 10 places

← Back to site

Hundru Falls

Scenic waterfall near Ranchi — one of Jharkhand's most famous cascades.

Dassam (Dasam) Falls

Huge multi-tiered waterfall on the Kanchi river near Ranchi.

Jonha Falls

Picturesque waterfall near Ranchi with natural pools.

Usri Falls

A beautiful waterfall near Giridih — popular picnic spot.

Netarhat

Hill station — famous for sunrise/sunset viewpoints (the 'Queen of Chotanagpur').

Baidyanath Dham (Deoghar)

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas — important pilgrimage site.

Parasnath Hill

Highest mountain in Jharkhand — important Jain pilgrimage site.

Jubilee Park (Jamshedpur)

Large city park built by Tata Steel, a green oasis in Jamshedpur.

Betla National Park (Palamu)

Wildlife park in Palamu Tiger Reserve — known for forests and historic forts.

Rajrappa Temple

Famous temple on the confluence of rivers — spiritual and scenic spot.
